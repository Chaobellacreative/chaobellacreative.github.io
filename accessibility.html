Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: March 2026 | Chaobella Creative LLC, Tampa, FL

Our Commitment

Chaobella Creative is committed to ensuring our website is accessible to people with disabilities. We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Measures We've Taken

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
  • ARIA labels on interactive elements (buttons, links, forms)
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios across text and backgrounds
  • Keyboard navigability — all interactive elements are reachable via Tab key
  • Focus indicators visible on all interactive elements
  • Alt text on meaningful images
  • Language attribute set on HTML element for screen readers
  • Mobile-responsive design for users on varying devices
  • Form fields include visible labels

Known Limitations

As a small business website, we may have areas that fall short of full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. We are actively working to identify and address any gaps. Portfolio video content may not currently include captions — we are working to add these.

Feedback & Contact

If you experience any accessibility barriers on our website or need content in an alternative format, please contact us:
